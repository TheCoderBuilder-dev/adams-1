A Vision That
Built History

It began with a sketch on paper and a dream that defied colonial expectations.

Abdul Habib Adam envisioned what would become East and Central Africa's first shopping centre. Against all odds, he purchased land, drew the designs himself, and brought in an architect from South Africa to make it official.

1954 Total Petrol Station — first structure
1959 Grand opening — 9 shops, 9 flats
1964 Metropole Cinema opens
Today 73 years strong, 40+ businesses
Post Office Original tenant since 1959
